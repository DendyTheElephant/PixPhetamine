\subsection{Detailed Description}
My Vertex\+Array\+Object structure to handle 3D models efficiently in V\+R\+AM. 

Allocate V\+B\+Os (buffers) to store vertices, normals and colors of the mesh ~\newline
 Then load mesh attributes to the G\+PU (V\+R\+AM) ~\newline
 Use the ID of the V\+BO to display from the V\+R\+AM ~\newline
 Then free ressources on the G\+P\+U!
