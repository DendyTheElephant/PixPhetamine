\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{struct_render_1_1_low_level_wrapper_1_1_g_buffer}{G\+Buffer} is a custom F\+BO (image buffers) to render the scene and apply some postprocess job. 

The \hyperlink{struct_render_1_1_low_level_wrapper_1_1_g_buffer}{G\+Buffer} is used to get the scene in the screen space, with colors, normals, depth, and type of objects in the screen. ~\newline
 Post process effects and deffered shading can be applied on the color texture (a V\+BO is also provided). Don\textquotesingle{}t forget to \hyperlink{struct_render_1_1_low_level_wrapper_1_1_g_buffer_a234a571bceff473b02ad8467bace7499}{free()} after using!
