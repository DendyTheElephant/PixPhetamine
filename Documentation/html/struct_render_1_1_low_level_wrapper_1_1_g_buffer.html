Detailed Description

GBuffer is a custom FBO (image buffers) to render the scene and apply some postprocess job.

The GBuffer is used to get the scene in the screen space, with colors, normals, depth, and type of objects in the screen.
Post process effects and deffered shading can be applied on the color texture (a VBO is also provided). Don't forget to free() after using!
